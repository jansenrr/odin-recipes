Ahi Poke

Pronunciation: "awh-hee" "poh-keh"

What makes a good poke bowl is simplicity

all it really needs is the poke and rice, and furikake if you want, nothing else

Ingredients

  • Sushi grade ahi (tuna)
  • Soy sauce (preferably Japanese)
  • Sesame oil
  • Hawaiian salt
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Sweet yellow onion
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Cut ahi into dices (about 1 inch cubes)
  2. Slice sweet yellow onion (about 1mm thick) and set aside
  3. Slice green onion (about 1mm thick) and set aside
  4. Grate garlic and ginger and combine in a bowl
  5. Combine all ingredients in a bowl
  6. Allow poke to chill in fridge for an hour

To Serve

  1. Put a bed of rice in a bowl (optional: add a layer of furikake on the rice)
  2. Place as much poke as you would like inside
  3. Have at it!
